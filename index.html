About

I am a research assistant at the RAI Lab, where I focus on applying foundational models and machine learning techniques to the segmentation of histopathology images. Alongside research, I contribute to the design and development of an AI-powered platform that assists pathologists in real-world diagnostics. I earned my M.Sc. in Software Engineering from Isfahan University of Technology (IUT), where I cultivated a strong foundation in algorithms, intelligent systems, and networked software. My academic and professional journey reflects a deep interest in image analysis, bioinformatics, data mining, and next-generation computer networks, including beyond 5G technologies. I am driven by the challenge of applying AI to solve complex, impactful problems at the intersection of healthcare, computing, and connectivity.
